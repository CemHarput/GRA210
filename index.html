The Beauty of Chess

The Beauty of Chess is a chess event that offers a perspective of the past, present and the future of interface design. From a semiotic perspective, the goal of the event is to make sense of tendencies towards more indexical user experiences rather than the iconic or symbolic trends.

Event is open to everyone but there are limited seats for the workshops. Those who register earlier will have the priority.May 3 - May 5, 2019, (Friday, Saturday and Sunday). From, Bilkent University, Odeon
